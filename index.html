the Barbican


Our building is temporarily closed. But we’re still with you. If

you find yourself in need of some inspiration – or distraction –

we’ll always have something for you to read, watch or listen to.

Please consider donating

We rely on the money we raise through ticket sales, commercial activities and fundraising to

deliver our arts and learning programme. It forms more than 60% of our income. Show

your support by making a donation and help inspire more people to discover and love the arts.
